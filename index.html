What do I mean by Classic Hip Hop?

This site will focus on mid 80s thru 90s hip hop music. More generally, 1985-2010 the first 25 years of Hip-Hop when recordings were widely available.

Classic Hip Hop includes these eras: Old School Hip Hop, Golden Age Hip Hop, The East vs. West, G-Funk Era, Shiny Suit/"jiggy" era, Bling/Crunk Era and more.

And includes these styles: Boom-Bap, Gangsta Rap, Mafioso Rap and more.
